About Gulab Seva Foundation

Gulab Seva Foundation brings hope, dignity, and opportunity to every life. We serve orphans, elderly, jobless youth, and underserved families, ensuring no one is left behind.

Our Mission

Building an India where everyone has a safe home, every child receives education, every elderly person is respected, and every deserving youth finds meaningful employment.

Our Vision

A society where no one is hungry, homeless, or helpless. Communities grow together, humanity is preserved, and every individual lives with dignity.

Opportunities & Programs

Safe Homes

Providing secure shelters to orphans, elderly, and families in need.

Education

Quality education and vocational training for every child and youth.

Healthcare

Access to medical care, free health checkups, and hospitals for all.

Employment

Creating sustainable jobs and livelihood opportunities for deserving youth.

Community Support

Building a network of followers, volunteers, and supporters to strengthen humanity.
