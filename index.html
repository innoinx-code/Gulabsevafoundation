About Gulab Seva Foundation

Gulab Seva Foundation is a registered charitable trust dedicated to helping orphans, elderly, jobless youth, and families in need. Our goal is to ensure shelter, education, healthcare, and livelihoods for all. The first 2 Crore followers will receive a unique GSF ID granting priority access to future benefits.

Our Mission

Build a self-sustaining society where no one sleeps hungry, every child is educated, and every individual has the dignity of life. Programs focus on free jobs, homes, healthcare, and education.

Our Vision
